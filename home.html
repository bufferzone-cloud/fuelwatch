Help & About

📖 Manual

1. Finding fuel stations: Use the interactive map or search bar. Tap any station icon to see distance, price, and amenities.

2. Navigation: Click "Navigate" on a station card. The app calculates fuel needed and draws a route.

3. Trip Planner: Enter destination → select fuel station → pick fun stops → review → draw route.

4. Garage & Fuel Logs: Add vehicles, track refueling history, view consumption trends.

5. Dark Mode: Toggle in sidebar for night driving.
