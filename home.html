Help & About

📖 Manual

1. Finding fuel stations: Use the interactive map or the search bar. Tap any station icon to see distance, price, amenities, and opening hours. You can also filter by fuel type or amenities using the chips above the map.

2. Navigation: Click "Navigate" on a station card. The app will calculate fuel needed based on your active vehicle, show ETA, and draw a green route on the map. Use the "Start Trip" button (appears after route is drawn) to get turn‑by‑turn instructions.

3. Trip Planner (4‑step wizard): Enter a destination → select a fuel station along the route → optionally pick a fun stop (restaurant, park, mall, zoo, farm, river, etc.) → review your trip summary and safety checklist → draw the route on map with color‑coded segments (green = to station, red = to destination).

4. Garage & Fuel Logs: Add multiple vehicles, set active car, track refueling history, and view consumption trends with a chart.

5. Reports & Community: Submit real‑time station status (fuel availability, price updates, queues) to help other drivers.

6. Dark Mode: Toggle in sidebar for night driving comfort.

🚀 About FuelRadar

FuelRadar is your ultimate fuel companion – designed by drivers for drivers. We combine live fuel station data from OpenStreetMap, intelligent route planning with fuel cost estimation, and community reports to make every journey smarter and cheaper.

Key features: Real‑time fuel prices, vehicle‑specific consumption, multi‑stop trip planner with fun place suggestions, export logs, favorites, and trip history.
