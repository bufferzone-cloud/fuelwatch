Help & Offline mode

📡 Offline capability

Station data is cached every 2 minutes. Even without internet, previously loaded stations remain available. Map tiles are also cached by your browser.

🚗 Smart navigation

When you tap "Navigate", the app calculates exactly how many litres your active vehicle needs based on distance and consumption, and shows ETA.

📝 Reporting

Each station shows its distance. Use the dropdown to report fuel availability, queues, or price updates. Reports are stored locally.

💾 Garage & logs

Manage multiple vehicles, track fuel logs, and see total spending. The active vehicle is used for navigation and calculator.
