Help & About

📖 Manual

1. Finding fuel stations: Use the interactive map or the search bar. Tap any station icon to see distance, price, amenities, and opening hours. Filter by fuel type or amenities using the chips above the map.

2. Navigation: Click "Navigate" on a station card. The app calculates fuel needed based on your active vehicle, shows ETA, and draws a green route. Use "Start Trip" for turn-by-turn instructions.

3. Trip Planner (4-step wizard): Enter destination → select a fuel station along route → optionally pick a fun stop (restaurant, park, mall, zoo, farm, river, viewpoint, museum, amusement park) → review summary and safety checklist → draw route with color-coded segments.

4. Garage & Fuel Logs: Add multiple vehicles, set active car, track refueling history, view consumption trends.

5. Reports & Community: Submit real‑time station status (fuel availability, price updates, queues).

6. Dark Mode: Toggle in sidebar for night driving.

🚀 About FuelRadar

FuelRadar is your ultimate fuel companion – designed by drivers for drivers. We combine live fuel station data from OpenStreetMap, intelligent route planning with fuel cost estimation, and community reports.
