Help & About

📖 Manual

1. Find stations: Use the map or search. Tap a station to navigate or report.

2. Garage: Add your vehicles, track fuel logs, see consumption trends.

3. Trip Planner: Enter destination, select a fuel station along the route, add fun stops, then draw route on map.

4. Reports: Submit station status (fuel available, price updates, etc.) to help the community.

5. Favorites & History: Save stations and review past trips.

6. Dark mode: Toggle in sidebar.

🚀 About FuelRadar

FuelRadar is your ultimate fuel companion – helping you find the cheapest and nearest fuel stations, plan trips with fuel cost estimates, and share real-time station reports. Built with ❤️ for drivers everywhere.
